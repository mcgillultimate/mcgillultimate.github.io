Unleashed and Untrained

Calling all new pups and bonafide huskies, this year's U&U will take place on Saturday, October XXnd and Sunday, October XXrd!

Uniting teams across Canada and the United States for 19 years, this fun and spirited 7v7 tournament boasts a long history of tail-wagging and tongue-lolling good times.

So grab a mixed team (up to 21 players per team) and join the pack! Contact mcgillultimate@gmail.com for more information and to reserve your team's spot. Bids are made on a first come, first served basis, so rally the troops!
