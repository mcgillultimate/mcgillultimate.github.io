McGill Ultimate Team

About

McGill Ultimate Team is a self-funded SSMU club (Students’ Society of McGill University) consisting of all four competing ultimate teams affiliated with the university: men’s A and B, and women’s A and B. We compete both provincially and nationally each year during the school’s fall semester, and are hoping to compete in the USAU series in the Spring. Tryouts occur at the beginning of September and practices are usually 4-6 hours of commitment each week of September and October.

Unleashed and Untrained

Unleashed and Untrained is an annual tournament hosted by the McGill Ultimate Team held in Montreal. This year the tournament will be at the Douglas Hospital Fields on October 27 and 28, 2018. The format is mixed-gender with 24 teams from Canada and the USA playing full-field, 7 on 7. Participation in the tournament includes:

  • Delicious Montreal bagels and fruit for breakfast on Saturday
  • BBQ lunch at the fields for lunch on Saturday
  • Huck, layout, and hot dog eating contests with prizes
  • A tournament party Saturday night
  • Tim Hortons donuts and coffee on Sunday morning
  • Trophy awarded to the winning team and a “Rowdy Dawg” Spirit award for the most spirited buncha dawgs

Visiting teams are invited to “billet” which means being hosted free of charge at a McGill player’s home to help keep travel costs down. Billeting will be coordinated once bids are set.

This year’s tournament fee will be $500 (US or CAN). Fees can be paid by check, cash, or e-mail interac transfer to [email]. A receipt will be sent out once payment has been received.

Bids for this tournament will be accepted on a first come, first serve basis. If you are interested in attending, please apply here.

We hope to see you here for U&U!
